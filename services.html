Research: In this initial stage, the designer needs to identify the website’s end goal, usually in close collaboration with the client or other stakeholders.
Prototyping: The prototyping provides the foundation for any well-designed website. It helps give designers a clear idea of the website’s information architecture and explains the relationships between the various pages and content elements.
Design: This part of the design process will often be shaped by existing branding elements, colour choices, and logos, as stipulated by the client. But it’s also the stage of the web design process where a good web designer can really shine.
